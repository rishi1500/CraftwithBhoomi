Our Story

Meet Bhoomi Ahuja

Every craft begins not with material, but with meaning. I'm Bhoomi Ahuja — a maker, a dreamer, and someone who truly believes the most precious gifts are the ones you can't find in any store.

What started as a quiet passion for art in a small studio has grown into something beautifully purposeful: creating keepsakes that families treasure for decades. Each piece carries the giver's heart and the receiver's story.

I don't make products. I make memories you can hold — woven from photographs, names, moments, and love.
